Here are some ways to reduce your carbon footprint:

Transportation

Drive less, walk or bike when possible, and use public transportation. You can also consider switching to an electric vehicle.

Energy

Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use, and lower the thermostat in the winter and summer. You can also take short showers, turn off the water while brushing your teeth or washing dishes, and don't leave your phone charging when it's full.
Waste
Reduce, reuse, repair, and recycle. Buy products with less packaging, in bulk, or buy second-hand.
Food
Eat more locally-grown or organic produce, less red meat, and throw away less food.
